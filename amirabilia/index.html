How It Works

Amirabilia builds unparalleled geo-located learning adventures that bring real-world and digital objects, places, and people to life. Engage in active learning through elements that challenge you to interact with your surroundings and experiment on-location.

Amirabilia’s digital content mixes with reality to create unique learning experiences for any age group, location, and subject. Engage your learners and challenge your visitors with an Amirabilia immersive learning experience.


Features

Location-based learning

Learning content is geo-tagged and unlocked when you visit the area.

Activities and challenges

Each experience is centered around a story with characters and activities.

Field log

A personal record of the places, people and items collected along the way.

Rewards

Earn badges and experience points when you complete activities or collect items.

Echoes

Voices from the past, present and future and other immersive sounds.

Mini quiz

Multiple choice quizzes encourage you to look around your surroundings for answers.

Treasure hunt

Characters challenge you to find something in the area and follow clues.

Augmented Reality

AR content can be unlocked on location and provides an augmented view of characters, buildings, objects or other 3D content.
